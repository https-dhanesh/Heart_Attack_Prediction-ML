This project will assist in prediction of heart attack to patient as well as to the physician

The "Heart Attack Prediction Application" is a digital tool designed to assist healthcare professionals and individuals in assessing the risk of a heart attack

  • Helps medical professionals and individuals in assessing the risk of a heart attack to identify heart attack early
  • Uses machine learning algorithm and patient health data to provide accurate predictions
  • This project will utilize a large dataset of medical records and validate with help of predictive algorithm
